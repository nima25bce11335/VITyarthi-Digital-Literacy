TASK 5 : Cybercrime Awareness Case Study & Prevention Guide

Part B – Prevention Checklist:

Stay Safe Online Checklist:
1. Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts and update them regularly.
2. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
3. Avoid clicking on suspicious links or downloading attachments from unknown sources.
4. Keep your devices updated with the latest security patches and antivirus software.
5. Be mindful of what you share on social media—avoid posting sensitive personal details.
6. For UPI/Online Payments: Always verify the recipient’s details before transferring money.
7. For UPI/Online Payments: Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone, even if they claim to be from your bank.
8. Report any suspicious activity or cybercrime immediately through the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) or call the Helpline 1930.
